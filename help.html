Here at Code Institute, we want Startup Academy to be a place where our members always feel safe in; therefore, we take all reports of harassment or abuse extremely seriously.

If you believe you are being harassed or you come across content that you consider it violates our values, please report this to us at startupacademy@condeinstitute.net.
