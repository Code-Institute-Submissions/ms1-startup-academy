Frequently Asked Questions

Contact us here if you don't see your question.

1. How much does the programme costs?

The Startup Academy is entirely free for Code Institute's students and graduates. All you need to join is a device with access to the internet.

2. How many individuals can participate in the programme?

We do not have a limit.

3. How much time should I expect to allocate for the programme?

There is a minimum commitment of 1:30 hours a week for the sprints.

4. Who can participate in the programme?

The Academy is open to all individuals. Entrepreneurs about to start building their startup can also go through the programme.

If you’re already working on a startup, you are more than welcome to participate as well.

5. If I’m already building a startup, why should I consider it?

If you’re building a startup, the programme will provide valuable insight into running your business through exposure to best practices, practical resources, and actionable guidance.

In addition to the sprint material, you will become part of a talented community of like-minded individuals.

6. Are there requirements to complete the programme?

There are no tests, exams or requirements to complete/pass the programme. However, we highly advise not to miss any of the sprints.

7. If I’m collaborating with other individuals, who should participate?

We recommend your co-founders/teammates to also participate in the programme.

8. Who is conducting the sprints?

Carlos, in collaboration with a great group of entrepreneurs and industry experts, will conduct the sprints.

9. Will the sprints be recorded?

No. However, all sprints materials will be shared.

10. What happens when the programme concludes?

Upon completing the Academy, you will have learned invaluable lessons that will exponentially increase your startup's probability of success.
